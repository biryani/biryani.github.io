Abhijith Jayakumar

Quantum ''and'' Computing

I am a CNLS Post-Doc at the Theoretical Division of the Los Alamos National Lab. My research is focussed broadly on topics at the intersection of Quantum Information, Machine Learning and Statistical Physics. Before Los Alamos, I did my Ph.D. focusing on quantum algorithms and M.Tech in computational science from the Indian Institute of Science.
